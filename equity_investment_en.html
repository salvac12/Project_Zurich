Project Details

Investment Structure

The investment is channelled through a SOCIMI (Spanish Listed Real Estate Investment Trust), which allows capital gains generated from the asset disposal to be exempt from corporation tax.

  • Tax advantage on capital gains through REIT structure
  • Investment horizon: 32 months (construction + stabilisation)
  • Target return: 13.5% IRR per annum
  • Land acquired with construction licence already approved
