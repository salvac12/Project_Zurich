Project ZURICH

EUR 21M "Build to Sale" short-term investment (32 months) in SOCIMI for affordable housing construction project in prime Madrid area.

Opportunity to invest up to a maximum of €20.2M in the construction and stabilization phase of an affordable rental residential building in Valdebebas, one of the areas with the highest growth and revaluation in Madrid.

The asset will benefit from Law 3/2024, which allows the conversion of office land to protected residential, generating value from the start.

Project Details

Investment Structure

The investment is channeled through a SOCIMI (Listed Company for Investment in the Real Estate Market), which allows capital gains generated by the sale of the asset to be exempt from corporate tax.

  • Tax advantage on capital gains through SOCIMI structure
  • Investment horizon: 32 months (construction + stabilization)
  • Target return: 13.5% annual IRR
  • Land acquired with construction license already approved
