Project ZURICH

BTR Investment Opportunity in Affordable Housing, Prime Madrid Location

Investment opportunity in an affordable residential development in Valdebebas, one of Madrid's fastest-growing and highest-appreciating districts.

We present two equity investment strategies tailored to different investment profiles: Build to Sale (short-term, 32-36 months) and Build to Rent (long-term, 15-year hold with subsequent liberalization).

The asset benefits from Law 3/2024, enabling conversion of office-zoned land to regulated residential use, unlocking value creation from inception through a SOCIMI (REIT) structure for tax optimization.
