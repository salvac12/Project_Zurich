Project ZURICH

EUR 42M senior financing facility structured under Project Finance scheme for affordable rental residential asset located in Valdebebas, Madrid's most dynamic district.

The facility benefits from a protective and adaptive covenant structure including minimum DSCR requirements, supported by regulated rental income and expected occupancy rates exceeding 97% during the protection period.

The loan is structured with a 30-year term featuring a 3-year principal moratorium period. Senior financing provides comprehensive credit enhancement through robust DSCR covenants ensuring sound lender protection.

Credit Enhancement Features

Substantial Equity Buffer
The SOCIMI investment vehicle contributes approximately €20M of equity capital, providing first-loss absorption and ensuring alignment of sponsor interests.
Prime Location Fundamentals
Valdebebas combines modern infrastructure, excellent transport connectivity, and structurally higher demand than available supply.
Regulated Income Stream
Protected rental regime provides predictable cash flows with built-in inflation adjustments and occupancy rate protection during stabilisation period.
Experienced Sponsor Team
Management team with proven track record in Spanish residential development and strong institutional relationships.
Pre-Approved Planning
Construction licence already obtained, significantly reducing planning and execution risk factors.
Institutional Exit Strategy
Asset suitable for institutional ownership upon stabilisation, providing multiple exit options for debt refinancing or asset disposal.

Project Finance Standard Protections

Security & Control
Asset pledge over project SPV shares and material assets; assignment of rental receivables and bank accounts with control agreements.
EPC & Step-in Rights
Fixed-price, date-certain EPC contract with performance guarantees; lender step-in rights and cure periods.
Covenants & Reserves
DSCR maintenance, distribution lock-up triggers, and funded reserve accounts (debt service, capex, contingencies).
